Biography

My Chinese name is 于(Yu)鹏(Peng)飞(fei). I am a PhD student in computer science at UIUC blender lab advised by Heng Ji. My research interests include information extraction (IE) and its applications in NLP. I also have some experience on a variety of topics, such as meta learning, transfer learning, continual learning, weakly-supervised learning and multimodal learning.
